    The Real Conversion Gap

    When a buyer clicks your listing, they silently ask:

    • Is this right for me?
    • Can I trust this seller?
    • Is it worth the price?
    • What if I regret it?

    Most descriptions fail because they:

    • List features without anchoring value
    • Force the buyer to interpret benefits alone
    • Ignore subconscious risk triggers
    • Overload mobile readers
    • End without a decisive direction

    Traffic without psychological structure creates hesitation. Hesitation kills conversion.
